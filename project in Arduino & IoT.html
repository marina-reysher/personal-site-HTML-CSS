Project in Arduino & IoT

We created a remote-controlled entertaining system for pets.
We used the Arduino platform to make sure that the pet is hydrated and entertained.
We built our application on the Xamarin platform. The user has the option to start from a distance the entertaining car or to pour the pet some water.The water dispenser has a button the pet can press and pour himself water.We used the Azure database to save data such as the number of times that the pet pressed the water button and the amount of water that he drank.Each button press triggered an https request that activated the car or the water dispenser.
